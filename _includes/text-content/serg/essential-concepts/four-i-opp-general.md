<p><span>Ideological Racism</span>: the belief that some racial groups are superior to others.</p>

<p><span>Interpersonal Racism</span>: interactions between two or more individuals that enact racism (e.g., racial epithets, exclusions, violence, microaggressions, etc.)</p>

<p><span>Internalized Racism</span>: when an individual endorses racist beliefs about themselves or members of their racial group(s)</p>

<p><span>Institutional Racism</span>: when systems and rules are designed to enact racial inequality or discrimination (e.g., discriminatory laws, disproportionate policing of some groups over others, education systems that privilege certain groups, etc.)</p>
