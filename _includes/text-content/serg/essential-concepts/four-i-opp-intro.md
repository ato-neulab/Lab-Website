<p>The “Four I's of Oppression'' was written by the Chinook Fund as a way of breaking down racism and other forms of oppression into distinct components. The Four I's encourage us to look beyond individual interactions and to incorporate a wider lens on how oppression operates within our society. Understanding what these 4 I’s empower designers to address racism (especially unintended biases and oppression) within their game systems and world building.</p>
