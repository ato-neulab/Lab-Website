<p>Prior to engaging with the SERG framework, it is important to set game design and final outcome goals. Given the volume of content within this toolkit–including the framework, activities, and reading material–it is easy to get lost, overwhelmed, or lose sight of the purpose of utilizing this resource. As such, approaching this work considering the intended outcome (e.g., revising an existing design, developing a new design, discussing the work with members of your team, etc.) is beneficial to maximizing this framework’s usability.</p>
