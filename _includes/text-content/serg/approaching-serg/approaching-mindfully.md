<p>Keep in mind that our collective understanding of race is constantly evolving – race/ethnicity/culture are all informed by global and local histories, power dynamics, and personal identities and experiences – there are likely to be disagreements and the language we use today may not incorporate the knowledge we have tomorrow.</p>
<p>
It is most important to use this framework with an open mind, the willingness to be vulnerable and acknowledge and repair mistakes, and the flexibility to adapt to your context when necessary.</p>
