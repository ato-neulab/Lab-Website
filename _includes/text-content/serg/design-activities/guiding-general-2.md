<p><span>Engagement with Target Audiences & Lived Experiences</span><br>
Players may feel enriched when engaging with something that resonates with themselves, such as through identity or lived experiences. Consider how your game might express cultural concepts that privileges the perspectives of different players.<br><br>

- What expectation do you have about someone's prior familiarity with the race and culture you are exploring? How might this add or lessen their experience?
- Who is going to play your game? What impressions or thoughts would they have in relation to your game’s genre or style?
- How do players engage with their experiences? Are there ‘in’ jokes or traditions that they are able to play into?</p>
