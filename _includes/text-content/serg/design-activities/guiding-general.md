<p><span>Engagement through Breadth vs Depth</span><br>
There are hundreds of distinct races, each an umbrella to dozens of unique cultures, languages, and traditions. You may resonate with one in particular or engage with many. Consider the extent to which you are going to express one or more races, cultures, and traditions.<br><br>

- Does your content center around an individual race, or is diversity a larger part of an overarching narrative? <br>
- How far are you diving into these lineages? To what extent are you exploring these in the game design process? <br>
- Is your attention focused on engaging with a specific ethnicity, or is it divided amongst an entire caste? <br>
- Sometimes, addressing race in breadth might prevent you in addressing race in depth. Where does your game fall upon this spectrum?</p>
