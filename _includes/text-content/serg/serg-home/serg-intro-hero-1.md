<p>The Spectrums for Engaging Race in Games, or SERG, framework is a collection of 8 spectrums informed by three essential concepts about race: The Four I’s of Oppression &Liberation, Critical Race Theory, and Designing for BIPOC Flourishing.</p>

<p>These spectrums are intended to be used as a starting point for designers when beginning their conversations surrounding how race is portrayed in their game and the implications of those choices.</p>
